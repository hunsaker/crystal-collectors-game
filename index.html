Instructions:

You will be given a GOAL number to reach at the start of the game. By clicking on a crystal a specifc number of points will add to your TOTAL SCORE.

You win the game by matching your TOTAL SCORE to the GOAL number, you lose the game if your TOTAL SCORE goes above the GOAL number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the games starts, the GOAL number and crystal values will change.
